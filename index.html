About FreeVoice

Our Mission

FreeVoice is designed to empower freedom of speech, press freedom, and human rights advocacy through secure, accessible peer-to-peer communication. We believe everyone deserves the ability to speak freely and connect globally, regardless of bandwidth limitations or geographic location.

Core Principles

  • Privacy First: Peer-to-peer connections mean your conversations never pass through our servers
  • Freedom of Expression: No censorship, no content filtering, no surveillance
  • Accessibility: Optimized for low-bandwidth connections in rural and restricted areas
  • Transparency: Open-source code available for audit and self-hosting
  • Security: Optional end-to-end encryption for sensitive communications

Technology

Built on WebRTC technology with inspiration from VDO.Ninja's architecture, FreeVoice provides:

  • Direct peer-to-peer video and audio connections
  • Multi-party conferencing capabilities
  • Screen sharing and recording features
  • OBS Studio integration for broadcasting
  • Adaptive bitrate for varying network conditions
